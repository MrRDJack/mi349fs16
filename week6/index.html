Shrimp Pasta

4 servings, prep 20min, cook 20min

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package linguini pasta
  • 1 pound large shrimp - peeled and deveined
  • 1tbsp salt
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 pound large shrimp - peeled and deveined

    • Cook pasta in boiling water. While pasta is cooking, saute onion and garlic in oil for about 5 minutes. Remove to bowl. Add shrimp to remaining drippings, and cook for about 3 minutes. Stir in artichoke hearts, wine, lemon juice, lemon peel, salt, pepper, and cooked onion. Heat to boiling over high heat. Reduce heat to low; simmer, covered, for 4 to 5 minutes. Toss with pasta and parsley. Serve immediately.

    Dumpling

    6 servings, prep 10min, cook 1hr 40min

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • 1/2 cup milk

    • Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in medium size bowl. Cut in butter until crumbly. Stir in milk to make a soft dough. Drop by spoonfuls into boiling stew. Cover and simmer 15 minutes without lifting lid. Serve.

    Mashed Patato

    4 servings, prep 10min, cook 5min

    Ingredients:

  • 3 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

    • Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain. Mash potatoes with milk, sour cream, salt, and pepper in the large pot.
